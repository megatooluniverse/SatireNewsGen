Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2024

1. Data Collection

We respect your privacy. Our Satire News Generator is a client-side web application. This means that all image processing and text rendering happen entirely within your internet browser.

2. Image Uploads

Any images you upload to the tool are never sent to or stored on our servers. They remain completely on your device.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies. We may use standard browser session storage simply to remember if you have agreed to our terms of service during your current visit, to prevent showing the warning popup repeatedly.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.
